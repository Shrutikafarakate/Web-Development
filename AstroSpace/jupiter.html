Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System. It is a gas giant known for its Great Red Spot.

  • Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun.
  • It has a strong magnetic field, much stronger than Earth’s.
  • The Great Red Spot is a giant storm that has raged for centuries.
  • Jupiter has at least 95 known moons, including Ganymede, the largest moon in the Solar System.
  • It has a very short day, rotating once every 10 hours.
  • Jupiter is composed mostly of hydrogen and helium.
  • It has faint rings made of dust particles.
  • Jupiter protects inner planets by attracting comets and asteroids with its gravity.
  • Juno is a NASA mission currently studying Jupiter’s atmosphere and magnetic field.
  • Jupiter radiates more heat than it receives from the Sun.
