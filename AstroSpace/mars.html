Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet due to its reddish appearance. It's the fourth planet from the Sun and a key focus of exploration for potential human colonization.

  • Mars has the tallest volcano in the Solar System, Olympus Mons.
  • It has two small moons: Phobos and Deimos.
  • Mars experiences seasons similar to Earth.
  • The planet has the largest dust storms in the Solar System.
  • Signs of ancient riverbeds suggest liquid water once flowed on Mars.
  • NASA’s Perseverance rover is currently exploring the surface of Mars.
  • Temperatures on Mars can drop to -125°C at night.
  • Mars has a very thin atmosphere, mostly made of carbon dioxide.
  • There are polar ice caps on Mars made of frozen carbon dioxide and water.
  • Future human missions aim to establish a permanent presence on Mars.
